## Summary

Adam Hart discusses the growing interest in optimizing videos for Google's AI Overviews. Rather than recommending new AI-specific tactics, he argues that successful AI SEO is fundamentally the same as good SEO: creating valuable, human-focused content. He also references Grizzle's research showing that branded SaaS YouTube channels appear in only a small percentage of AI Overview results, highlighting the importance of producing genuinely useful educational content.

---

## Key Insights

- AI SEO is essentially good SEO focused on delivering value to real people.
- Creating content for humans is more effective than trying to optimize specifically for AI systems.
- Most B2B SaaS YouTube channels currently receive very limited visibility within Google's AI Overviews.
- Educational, high-quality content remains the strongest long-term strategy for search visibility.

---

## Why This Matters

As AI-powered search becomes increasingly common, many marketers are searching for shortcuts to rank in AI-generated results. Adam emphasizes that sustainable visibility still depends on producing helpful, authoritative content rather than attempting to manipulate AI algorithms. This reinforces the importance of content quality for long-term B2B YouTube and SEO success.

---

## Possible Applications

- Focus on producing educational videos that answer real customer questions.
- Build YouTube content around search intent rather than AI-specific optimization tactics.
- Maintain strong SEO fundamentals when planning B2B video content.
- Prioritize audience value and expertise to improve long-term discoverability.
